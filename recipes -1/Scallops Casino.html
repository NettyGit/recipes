scallops-casino

Description & Cooking Time

This is a faster, and less expensive lasagna.

Prep: 20 mins

Cook: 18 mins

Total: 38 mins

Servings: 4

Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • ½ cup plain bread crumbs
  • 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 slices lean bacon
  • ½ pound sea scallops
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • ¼ green bell pepper, cut into thin strips
  • 1 tablespoon minced onion
  • 1 tablespoon chopped roasted red bell peppers
  • 1 large clove garlic, minced
  • ¼ cup dry white wine
  • 2 teaspoons chicken broth
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • ½ lemon, cut into wedges

Steps

  • Step 1 Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  • Step 2 Combine bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, olive oil, and parsley in a bowl.
  • Step 3 Cook bacon in a skillet over medium heat until golden but not crisp, 5 to 7 minutes. Transfer bacon to a paper towel-lined plate. Drain bacon fat from skillet until there is about 1 tablespoon fat left in the skillet. Add scallops and cook until golden brown, 1 1/2 to 2 minutes per side. Remove scallops from skillet.
  • Step 4 Wipe fat from skillet and add butter; cook and stir green bell pepper, onion, roasted red peppers, and garlic until fragrant and tender, 2 to 3 minutes. Add white wine; cook and stir for 1 minute. Add chicken broth and simmer for 1 minute; season with salt and pepper.
  • Step 5 Mix bacon and scallops into bell pepper mixture and transfer to a baking dish; top with bread crumb mixture.
  • Step 6 Bake in the preheated oven for 5 to 6 minutes. Turn on oven's broiler and broil until bread crumb mixture is lightly browned, 1 to 2 minutes.
